Battle Modes

Choose your weapon.

⚔️
Argument Mode
Users propose destinations and argue. Type /battle Paris vs Tokyo to trigger the AI judge instantly.
🎯
Challenge Mode
AI generates surprise challenges every few minutes. "Find a destination under £200" — who can find it first?
🎭
Travel Personalities
On joining, AI assigns you a personality — Budget Pirate, Luxury Escapist, Backpacking Goblin — shaping the judge's commentary.
🏆
Leaderboard
Every AI verdict awards a point. Climb the rankings across battles to become the ultimate travel champion.

How to use the chat

Commands you can use in the TravelBattle room:

/battle Paris vs Tokyo vs Barcelona Trigger an AI battle between any number of destinations
/suggest weekend getaways Ask AI to suggest destinations based on your prompt
Just chat normally! Mention budget, mood, weather — AI adds fun commentary
